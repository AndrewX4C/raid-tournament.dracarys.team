General rules

  • Format: League Play
  • Difficulty: Regular Raid
  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Start: Tuesday, February 21, 2023, at Reset Time
  • End: Monday, April 3, 2023 9am GMT

Team rules

  • You can choose your teammates
  • You can be a member of a single team only
  • Team can consist up to 5 players, so someone can be a substitute player (for the entire recorded run)

Video submission rules

  • Each week you have 6 days since Tuesday Reset time 9am GMT to submit a video or stream replay link (YouTube, Twitch, etc.) of your single best run for the week
  • Video link with your best run needs to be submitted 24 hours before the next weeks’ Reset time, so by
    Monday 9am GMT / 4am ET / 3am CT / 1am PT
  • The link needs to be submitted via the Tournament Website
  • We don’t accept direct video uploads due to technical limitations
  • If you don’t submit a video for any given week, you get 0 points for that week

Video review rules

  • Videos will be judged by the Tournament Organizers and points will be awarded in several categories
  • Video needs to include raid starting screen and raid completed message
  • Raid time starts when the names pop up in the top right corner after loading screen and ends when MiniMap gets fuzzy after Cerberus kill
  • Boss fight time starts when the names pop up in the top right corner after loading screen and ends with the final red hitmarker

Points system overview

  • A team with the most points wins the Tournament
  • In case of a tie at the end of the Tournament, there will be a playoff week only for the teams with the same amount of points
  • If playoff results in a tie in points, the fastest run wins
  • There are 75 points to get each week!

Points system categories

Raid time

  • Sub 40 min: 20 points
  • Sub 45 min: 15 points
  • Sub 50 min: 10 points
  • Sub 55 min: 5 points
  • 55+ min: 0 points

Note: points don’t stack, if you got e.g. 40 min run, you get 20 points

Number of deaths per Team per run

  • No deaths: 20 points
  • 1 death: 15 points
  • 2 deaths: 10 points
  • 3 deaths: 5 points
  • 4 and more deaths: 0 points

Important: death by red lines or “lava elevator” to fast travel doesn’t count.

If you die at any other point - it counts as 1 death per player.

For example a boss wipe means 0 points in that category.

Baal time to kill challenge

  • Sub 25 seconds: 5 points
  • Sub 30 seconds: 4 points
  • Sub 35 seconds: 3 points
  • Sub 40 seconds: 2 points
  • Sub 45 seconds: 1 point
  • Over 45 seconds: 0 points

Gargoyle lightning doors challenge

How many times did the team need to close doors (vents/flaps) before killing the boss
  • No doors closed*: 10 points
  • 1 time doors closed: 5 points
  • 2 times or more doors closed: 0 points

* meaning a kill on the first center

Quantum Computer Phases challenge

A Phase - a round of hacks of nodes to vent out the gas

  • 0 Phases (no hack): 10 points
  • 1 Phase (1 hack): 5 points
  • 2 Phases (2 hacks): 2 points
  • More than 2 hacks: 0 points

Cerberus number of switches challenge

  • No switch: 10 points
  • 1 switch: 5 points
  • 2 or more switches: 0 points

Fair play rules

  • No cheating hardware or software (like Cronus Zen)
  • No freezing bosses
  • No DMR glitch
  • No disconnections to fast travel!
  • You are allowed to use pro controllers (SCUF, DualSense Edge™, etc.) the same way as major competitive leagues allow it.
  • Next-gen console (PS5, Xbox Series X) has to host in case of a mixed lobby.
